About me

I'm a designer driven by curiosity, passion and a desire to discover the world around me.

In 2019 I obtained my bachelor degree in Product Design at Politecnico di Milano.

I continued my education by doing my Master Degree in Integrated Product Design, which allowed me to acquire skills at a multidisciplinary level, ranging from social research, through concept and idea development, to product engineering. I have developed projects to improve the company's communication and strategy, both digital and non-digital, and have broadened my skills in communication design. I have developed service design projects in order to improve the experience of the target users.

My education and work experiences carried out, allowed me to indentify as my main interest the subjects about project planning:I've learnt to develop my projects with design thinking and creative problem solving metodologies, on which I chose to develop my master's thesis.

Design is..

Designing does not mean only to project.
It means having curious eyes.
People appreciate what they are in they can identify and succeed you really have to communicate speak a common language, that it has to break down the borders, get around the prejudices and clichés, to get straight to the heart.
To be able to do this is need to put in place two fundamental processes: empathy and listening.
In the true understanding of the needs of others, there is the ingredient secret that allows us to do innovation, to find solutions a measure of people we are working for.
